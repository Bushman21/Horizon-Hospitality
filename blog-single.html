Ethiopia Rises Hotel

Welcome to Ethiopia Rises Hotel 🌿🏞️

Discover a serene escape along the Baro River, a picturesque tributary of the Grand Renaissance Dam, at Baro Bliss Hotel. Our hotel combines modern comfort with Ethiopia’s breathtaking natural beauty, offering elegantly designed rooms, warm hospitality, and panoramic views of lush riverbanks.

Whether you’re seeking a peaceful retreat, an adventure in Ethiopia’s landscapes, or a perfect spot for relaxation and reflection, Baro Bliss Hotel promises an unforgettable stay. Enjoy riverside dining, scenic walking trails, and thoughtful amenities that make every moment memorable.

Experience the harmony of nature and luxury — where your comfort meets Ethiopia’s stunning vistas.

Experience Comfort at Ethiopia Rises Hotel

Step into a place of elegance and convenience at Ethiopia Rises Hotel, where modern amenities meet warm Ethiopian hospitality. Located in a prime spot, the hotel offers beautifully appointed rooms, gourmet dining, and easy access to local attractions.

Perfect for both business travelers and leisure seekers, your stay here promises relaxation, comfort, and a touch of Ethiopian charm.
